Set a start and end, then drag the line to bend the route wherever you like. Share the result with a link.

Tip: once a route is drawn, grab any point on the line and drag — it adds a stop and reshapes the path. Drag the A/B pins to move the ends.

Please be advised that the graphical maps are provided for reference purposes only and may not fully correspond to the route specified in the permit.

In case of any discrepancies, the written route description contained in the permit shall prevail and be considered the official and legally binding route.

Users are required to review and follow the written route description prior to commencing transportation.
